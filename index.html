Vasilis Kontonis

Hi! I am a PhD student at the Computer Science Department of University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Professor Christos Tzamos. I am particularly interested in algorithms and theoretical machine learning. Currently, I am working on problems related to efficient robust inference in high dimensions.

Before coming to UW-Madison, I studied Electrical and Computer Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens where I was very lucky to be advised by Professor Dimitris Fotakis. You can grab a copy of my thesis in Greek here.
